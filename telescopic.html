Telescopic EP

Jul 6, 2024

Tracklist:

  1. Orthographic
  2. Trajectory
  3. Groove Voyage
  4. Away

Artwork by Rossman

I wanted to make another EP and it started with the song Trajectory. The chords were inspired by Rude Buster from the Deltarune OST (I've never played Undertale but I like the music). Groove Voyage was kind of my attempt to make another song like Groove Adventure, and while it ended up being pretty cool, it still didn't really live up to Groove Adventure.

Here's a screenshot of the project for Trajectory. I tried color coding stuff, but didn't really stick with it. I learned more about music production in general and I feel like I improved my mixing/mastering skills for this project.
